Understanding in one paragraph:

Joy Buolamwini's work reminded me that machine "vision" reflects the choices, omissions, and power structures of its creators. What looks like a neutral algorithm often invisibilizes people who were not represented in the data, amplifying harms. Unmasking those failures — through testing, documentation, and inclusive practice — is the first necessary step toward designing AI systems that recognize and respect all people.
